Course Overview

The B.Tech in Software Engineering at Goa University is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in the principles of software development, programming, and engineering practices. This course is ideal for students interested in pursuing a career in software development, systems engineering, or IT management.

Key Modules

Throughout the program, students will study a variety of essential subjects including:

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Software Design and Architecture
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Software Testing and Quality Assurance
  • Agile Methodologies
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Web Development and Cloud Computing
  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

Admission Information

To apply for the B.Tech Software Engineering course at Goa University, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • A completed high school education with a focus on mathematics and science.
  • A valid score in the Goa University Entrance Examination (GUEE) or equivalent national entrance exams.
  • English proficiency and good communication skills.

For detailed admission procedures, please visit the Goa University Admission Page.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the Software Engineering course at Goa University can explore a wide range of career opportunities, including:

  • Software Developer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Network Engineer
  • Project Manager
  • Quality Assurance Engineer
